What is Moocs ?

Moocs stands for Masive Open Online Courses
imagine you have University in your home on your PC or Mac instead of that one in the real world, not just that you will learn what ever you want from the best Universities ever such as
Harvard, MIT, Yale, and so on
and at the top of all that you get it free! and sometimes you can get a certificate prove that you've finished this content of the course
there are many of websites can offer you a lot of courses in any field you can imagine and we get you most populer websites in Programming and you can find on them courses in other fields as well

How to start ?

dealing with this websites is pretty easy, all what you need to do is to create an account on this website, search for the course you need, enroll the course, and you will find it on your dashboard where you can find all the courses you enrolled
and to choose the suitable course for you, read first the course info where they will tell you whether you need a specific background or not and what you will learn during this course
most of the courses you find is self-based which means you can watch them when ever you want but some courses putting deadlines for the assignment in order to earn its certificate
and speaking about certificate some websites offer it for free in some courses and others need to pay money but also you can ask for financial assistant which allow you to take it free if you can't pay its cost
